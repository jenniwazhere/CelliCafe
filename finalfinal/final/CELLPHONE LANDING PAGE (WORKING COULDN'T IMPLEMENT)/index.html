About Our App

The next iPhone is retailing at almost $1000. With expensive smart phones nowadays you want a good deal on your next smart phone. Buy a used phone and use our app to find out the best time to buy or even sell your next smart phone. Currently we support the two most popular phones, the iPhone 7 and Galaxy S7. More phones to be supported in the future.
